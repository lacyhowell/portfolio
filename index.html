About Me

I enjoy spending time outdoors, especially hiking, camping, and exploring new trails with my family. Outdoor activities give me an opportunity to relax, experience new places, and create lasting memories. Building this website allows me to combine those interests with what I am learning about HTML, CSS, accessibility, and organized web design.

My goal is to continue improving my web development skills throughout this course while creating a portfolio that demonstrates my progress. As I add more pages and features, I want the site to remain easy to navigate, visually consistent, and useful on both desktop and mobile devices. In future units, I plan to add more detailed projects and continue improving the site's design and interactivity.
